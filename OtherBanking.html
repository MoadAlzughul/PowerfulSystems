PR90 Plus

PR90 Plus An intelligent printer with ultimate performance, it fits in the teller desks or for any industry where fast transaction and safe print is mandatory. In addition, the printer is suitable to personalize checks. The printer is user-friendly, easy to maintain, and has an LCD display for two lines of 20 characters each. Extremely flexible for connectivity, PR90 Plus comes with options of the parallel interface, dual serial ports, USB, and LAN.

PR4 DR

PR4 DR is the highest performing printer in its class and has three stations to print receipts, journals, and transaction slips. Easy paper management, low noise level, and high operating speeds give top productivity. PR4 DR processes check with capability for MICR recognition (CMC7 and E13B) and are the only printer with automatic slip alignment and paper roll loading. The printer is ideal for the retail environment, hospitality, ticketing, and banks.

Cheque Deposit Kiosk

Cheque Deposit Kiosk Quick & easy to install and service. Display proper screen message to the customer. Increased customer satisfaction. Reduce customer churn. Easy Cheque Deposit.

ScanBox - Kiosk Network

ScanBox by Digital Check is a versatile, secure platform that serves as the central building block for kiosks, self-service stations, and other customer-facing applications. Featuring a CheXpress kiosk scanner, a SecureLink by Digital Check module for connecting to networks, and a touchscreen interface, then it can capture and store up to 300 checks or money orders in a secure metal enclosure. ScanBox can be used as a standalone, self-driving device, or it can be connected with other hardware and banking systems as part of a multi-faceted solution. It is commonly used in tandem with a cash-handling component to create simple yet effective kiosks that can handle the majority of self-service transactions.

LSA Range

LSA range of readers/sorters with speed from 130 to 380dpm is a combination of superior technology and robust design. The open transport path provides a reliable feeding mechanism for jam-free transport. It allows the flexibility of processing mixed documents such as checks, vouchers, meal tickets, receipts, and other documents that require data retrieval, encoding, printing, or sorting. MICR reading can be enhanced with the OCR recognition and options for UV, IR, and Watermark detection are available. The sorter is manufactured with two or twelve pockets sorting modules and is extendable to 48 pockets.

LA Range

The LA range integrates innovative technology with speed to process high volumes of up to 48k documents per hour. LA range with speeds from 300 to 800 DPM enables the processing of mixed documents. The dual-screen allows control of the application and quality of scanned images. The feeder with a capacity of 3200 documents can be refilled while in the process to reduce the machine s idle time. Equipped with three inkjet print heads, LA prints up to 5 lines per printhead. The efficient fraud detection is performed automatically without any delays or interruptions of processing the documents yielding high ROI.
