Digiflex Digiflex is the perfect solution for high-quality, cost-effective, black, and white variable data production printing. The combined technology of magnetography and cool xenon flash fusion supports the handling of a wide range of materials including paper, labels, plastics, foils, and other thermal sensitive substrates from 40gsm to 300gsm. The cool xenon flash fusion keeps the substrates stress free and maintains the dimensions during processing. It can be used in multiple configurations of Simplex, Single-Engine Duplex or Twin Engine Duplex and roll-to-roll, roll-to-cut, or roll-to-stack as per customer requirements. Suitable for a wide range of applications such as on-demand, transactional printing, security, MICR documents, identification labels, complex barcodes, and others.
