Check Scanners

Proactive banks are transforming customer experiences with the remote deposit check scanners. RDC is offering businesses convenience, saves time and costs while banks are effectively increasing their foot print with optimal costs.

CX30

CX30 is the first check scanner designed for Remote Deposit Capture and is the best-selling model of Digital Check for last 10 years. Affordable and durable for small volumes, the scanner capabilities include capturing check image with MICR and OCR, straight or return feed for duplex scanning with options of inkjet endorsement and UV check capture. Suited for a teller window, the scanner is used by half a million small businesses worldwide. The Best Read API facilitates the selection of the highest-quality image from several images captured simultaneously.

TS240

The multi-feed high-speed check scanner is versatile for teller window or remote deposits with high volumes. Known as workhorse scanner, it delivers reliability and superb maintenance in a simple and durable design. It is compatible with modular plug-ins for card reading or receipt printing and handles stacks of 100 checks to save time. Best Read system selects the best of four images to produce the highest CAR/LAR and Clear by Digital Check repairs the most difficult image. The UV version features a camera to capture UV security features and the optical light in one pass without reducing speed.

SmartSource Micro Elite

Simple, compact, and affordable, the single-feed scanner is perfect for RDC. At 45 documents per minute, it is durable and offers great performance with minimal maintenance. Symbolizing its name, the Micro Elite is 7x4 inches in size and comes as serial embedded with API and optional inkjet printer. Versatile to scan checks and money orders, it accepts ID cards and captures few items in color.

Smart Source Elite

The affordable high-speed scanner with a speed of 55 documents per minute is ideal for remote deposit and merchant capture solutions. With a 100-document capacity, the scanner is available as Serial Embedded and optional inkjet endorser. The compact and robust scanner is maintenance-free and delivers high performance with reliability. Elite 55 is a considerable option for small businesses with check volumes of 10-20 per day and even scans ID cards.

Teller Scan TS240

Teller Scan TS240,The multi-feed high-speed check scanner is versatile for teller window or remote deposits with high volumes. Known as workhorse scanner, it delivers reliability and superb maintenance in a simple and durable design. It is compatible with modular plug-ins for card reading or receipt printing and handles stacks of 100 checks to save time. Best Read system selects the best of four images to produce the highest CAR/LAR and Clear by Digital Check repairs the most difficult image. The UV version features a camera to capture UV security features and the optical light in one pass without reducing speed.

TS500

The teller scanners include modern features such as ID card image capture Auto alignment of checks Automatic Cleaning function TS500 paired with SecureLink allows teller capture over branch network or with decentralized workstations. It offers production speed scanning at teller windows even with a small desktop footprint. The optional modules include card reader, USB hub, thermal receipt printer paired with a camera, and auto-alignment to prevent paper jams. The self-cleaning system retains the peak performance and the SmartLED indicates when it is time for cleaning.

SmartSource Pro Elite

The teller capture with all great features of the Pro Elite series is smaller and faster with speed up to 155 documents per minute. The compact Pro Elite handles any job in the branch with no noise and is fit for deployment as a high-volume RDC scanner. The scanner with a four-line inkjet endorser is also available as optional UV capture with speed up to 80 documents per minute. Pro elite with large output pocket and double detection consume only 20 watts. The standard features are an auto-feeder, MICR reader, easy remote deposit capture applications, and superior image quality. Self-cleaning mode and front ID card capture will be added soon.

SmartSource Professional

Smart Source Professional is suitable for easy and fast integration for a teller or remote deposit applications. It is one of the few desktop scanners with two pockets sorting capability with the speed of 200 documents per minute. Auto-feeder, MICR reader, easy RDC applications, and superior image quality are some features of the Professional. Professional sorts documents into the dual exit pockets based on parameters set by the user. It maintains full speed during the sorting process and is a rare feature for scanners. Inkjet endorser prints 4 lines on the back of the check and optional front franker prints the checks as deposited. The rapid cleaning mode allows easy cleaning without removing external covers.

SmartSource Expert Elite

SmartSource Expert Elite, The newest in the SmartSource series, the Expert Elite is available as a network-ready model. The compact and high-speed scanner is PC-independent and ideal for virtual environments. Thin Client software makes it suitable for virtual environments like Citrix, VMWare, and others. The built-in processor and memory run the API files and resolve compatibility issues. Key functions such as MICR/OCR reading, image formatting, and compression are completed onboard before sending the image to the workstation. The traditional USB scanner can be converted to operate as an Ethernet model.

SmartSource Expert

The Expert includes all features of Professional series in addition to the built-in processing power for virtual infrastructure. The only network scanner with two-pocket sorting is available at speeds of 80 or 120 documents per minute. All key functions are completed internally before transmitting images to the workstation. The expert s capability to store 1000 images in memory offers a buffer for network interruptions. A backlit LCD displays the status or error codes.

SmartSource Expert Micro Elite

The first single-feed scanner with built-in network technology allows browser-based control from any connected PC or handheld devices such as smartphones, tablets, or touchscreens. It measures the size of a postcard and features ID card capture with a speed of 45 documents per minute. It is an OS-independent suitable for thin-client workstations and virtual desktop environments. The SecureLink2.0 allows browser-based control from any PC or handheld devices. The Serial Embedded model gives maximum compatibility with zero setup options for users. It also features an optional inkjet printer.

SecureLink Network Attached Scanning

SecureLink is the network protocol to connect the scanner to any network device. The tiny device is capable to operate the scanner instead of a desktop PC and control from an ordinary web browser. The scanner is operating system independent and operated from an internet browser, making it ideal for thin client workstations connected to a central server. The new version 2.0 supports SmartSource Expert Elite and Expert Micro Elite along with TellerScan and CheXpress. The streamlined commands feature allows easier integration and supports ID capture. The external device linked to existing TellerScan or CheXpress can make them a network-enabled device.

All Purpose Check and Document Scanner

Adaptive is the only scanner to scan checks and full-page documents as a mixed batch. A convenient scanner to process checks accompanied by documents with features of the flatbed. The updated Adaptive 2.0 reaches speeds of 105 DPM for checks, 78 DPM for full-sized pages, and 65 DPM for legal-sized sheets. The scanner considers each document as a separate entity and sustains the speed for mixed batches of documents.

SmartSource Micro Adaptive

The compact design of the Micro Elite with an over-sized image sensor of the Adaptive, the Micro Adaptive delivers MICR check capture with the ability to scan invoices, purchase orders, and other documents. It is Serial Embedded with ID card scanning ability and optional single-line endorser. The scanner communicates with the host PC over a USB cable using streamlined commands for faster integrations and installations. At just 5 inches wide, the Micro Adaptive captures documents in an upright position. In addition to MICR recognition and scanning of mixed documents, it has a slot to scan ID cards.

CX30 - TS240 UV

The CheXpress CX30 was designed for remote deposit applications for small business and merchant usage, but also for use in lower volume teller applications. While continuing Digital Check s track record of the highest image quality, MICR accuracy, and dependability found in every other DCC scanner, the CX30 brings all of those features in a more affordable offering for the business processing lower check volumes. Ideal for low volume application (less than 200 checks per day), the CX30 has the durability to process thousands of checks per day as well.

Back Counter Reader Sorter - Quantum DS

A powerful processor for all branch and back-office needs without massive footprint or maintenance budget. It ranks among the fastest small form, mass-market reader sorter with the speed of 200 DPM. The 300-item feeder with continuous feed capability has 12 pockets of 200-item capacity. The pockets can be programmed to sort the biggest batches according to business rules.

ScanBox - Kiosk Network-Enabled Check Scanning

The central building block for kiosks, self-service stations, and other customer-facing applications, ScanBox is versatile and secure. Equipped with CX30, a touch screen interface, and SecureLink to connect to the networks, it can store up to 300 checks in a metal enclosure. ScanBox can communicate either over Ethernet, WiFi, or even the tablet s optional 3G or 4G capability. It can be used as a desktop standalone or as part of a multi-faceted solution and handles much of image processing so checks are ready for clearing when exported to the network.

SmartSource Micro Elite SE

The SmartSource Expert Micro Elite is Digital Check s first single-feed check scanner with built-in network technology. The embedded processor running our SecureLink 2.0 API allows browser-based control from any connected PC or handheld device, including tablets, smartphones, and other touchscreens. It is an ideal solution for networked Remote Deposit Capture (RDC) or specialty branch situations.

SmartSource Micro Adaptive SE

The SmartSource Micro Adaptive is a single-feed scanner that can handle checks AND full-sized documents up to U.S. Letter size (8.5? x 11?). Based around the compact design of our Micro Elite series, but with the oversized image sensor of our popular SmartSource Adaptive, this little powerhouse delivers full-featured MICR check capture along with the ability to scan any invoices, purchase orders, or other documentation that goes along with a check. Each Micro Adaptive also comes with a straight-through path for scanning ID cards, and an optional single-line inkjet can print endorsements on the backs of your checks. The Micro Adaptive is a Serial Embedded (SE) scanner only at this time see below for details.

SmartSource Elite 55 SE

The SmartSource Elite 55 is an affordable high-speed scanner for remote deposit and merchant capture solutions. With a 100-document capacity and a top speed of 55 documents per minute, it s ideal for higher-volume remote deposit capture customers who may prefer a multi-feed scanner. The new SmartSource Elite 55 is similar to the previous Merchant Elite scanner, but with a single-line Inkjet endorser. The Elite 55 is also available with Serial Embedded technology, either with or without an inkjet endorser.

SmartSource ReceiptNow

A small desktop device to fit underneath the check scanner, Receipt Now prints the receipts for all transactions. The stacking with the scanner saves space and reduces clutter on the teller s desk. It is a thermal printer is faster with no cartridges or ribbons and with minimal maintenance of refilling, saving cost.

SmartSource ReceiptNow Elite

ReceiptNow Elite fits the SmartSource Elite, Expert Elite scanners and TellerScan TS500 for easy and faster printing of receipts. Available with Ethernet configuration, it is suitable for network and virtual environments. The thermal printer operates in continuous feed and faster in comparison to the ink-based printers.
