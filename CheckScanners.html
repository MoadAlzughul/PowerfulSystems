Check Scanners

Proactive banks are transforming customer experiences with the remote deposit check scanners. RDC is offering businesses convenience, saves time and costs while banks are effectively increasing their foot print with optimal costs.

Remote Deposit Capture

CX30 is the first check scanner designed for Remote Deposit Capture and is the best-selling model of Digital Check for last 10 years. Affordable and durable for small volumes, the scanner capabilities include capturing check image with MICR and OCR, straight or return feed for duplex scanning with options of inkjet endorsement and UV check capture. Suited for a teller window, the scanner is used by half a million small businesses worldwide. The Best Read API facilitates the selection of the highest-quality image from several images captured simultaneously.

Teller Scan

Teller Scan TS240,The multi-feed high-speed check scanner is versatile for teller window or remote deposits with high volumes. Known as workhorse scanner, it delivers reliability and superb maintenance in a simple and durable design. It is compatible with modular plug-ins for card reading or receipt printing and handles stacks of 100 checks to save time. Best Read system selects the best of four images to produce the highest CAR/LAR and Clear by Digital Check repairs the most difficult image. The UV version features a camera to capture UV security features and the optical light in one pass without reducing speed.

Network-Ready Devices

SmartSource Expert Elite, The newest in the SmartSource series, the Expert Elite is available as a network-ready model. The compact and high-speed scanner is PC-independent and ideal for virtual environments. Thin Client software makes it suitable for virtual environments like Citrix, VMWare, and others. The built-in processor and memory run the API files and resolve compatibility issues. Key functions such as MICR/OCR reading, image formatting, and compression are completed onboard before sending the image to the workstation. The traditional USB scanner can be converted to operate as an Ethernet model.

Full-Page Scanners

SmartSource Adaptive, Adaptive is the only scanner to scan checks and full-page documents as a mixed batch. A convenient scanner to process checks accompanied by documents with features of the flatbed. The updated Adaptive 2.0 reaches speeds of 105 DPM for checks, 78 DPM for full-sized pages, and 65 DPM for legal-sized sheets. The scanner considers each document as a separate entity and sustains the speed for mixed batches of documents.

Specialty Scanners

The CheXpress CX30 was designed for remote deposit applications for small business and merchant usage, but also for use in lower volume teller applications. While continuing Digital Check s track record of the highest image quality, MICR accuracy, and dependability found in every other DCC scanner, the CX30 brings all of those features in a more affordable offering for the business processing lower check volumes. Ideal for low volume application (less than 200 checks per day), the CX30 has the durability to process thousands of checks per day as well.

Serial Embedded Scanners

The SmartSource Expert Micro Elite is Digital Check s first single-feed check scanner with built-in network technology. The embedded processor running our SecureLink 2.0 API allows browser-based control from any connected PC or handheld device, including tablets, smartphones, and other touchscreens. It is an ideal solution for networked Remote Deposit Capture (RDC) or specialty branch situations.

Peripherals

A small desktop device to fit underneath the check scanner, Receipt Now prints the receipts for all transactions. The stacking with the scanner saves space and reduces clutter on the teller s desk. It is a thermal printer is faster with no cartridges or ribbons and with minimal maintenance of refilling, saving cost.
